Our Story

SR Tilapia Fingerlings has been in business for over 5 years, helping fish farmers achieve success with high-quality, sex-reversed male tilapia fingerlings.

Many clients struggled to achieve their harvest goals, so we upgraded our approach—offering online tools and expert guidance to ensure our clients’ success.

Mission & Vision

  • Mission: Provide quality fingerlings and hands-on support for every fish farmer’s success.
  • Vision: Be the leading provider and educator in tilapia aquaculture, making farming easier through technology.

Why Choose Us?

  • 5 years of experience in tilapia aquaculture
  • Proven results and satisfied clients
  • Dedicated to your farm’s success, every step of the way
